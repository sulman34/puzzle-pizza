ABOUT US

Satisfy Your Cravings with Puzzle Pizza's Premium Ingredients

Welcome to Puzzle Pizza, where we believe that every great pizza starts with the finest ingredients. Our journey began in 2008 when our founder, a passionate foodie and puzzle enthusiast, combined his love for both by creating unique puzzles made of pizza dough.
After receiving rave reviews from friends and family, he decided to take his puzzle concept to the next level by opening Puzzle Pizza. We are proud to offer not only delicious and freshly made pizzas but also a fun and interactive dining experience.
Our team of talented chefs carefully curates each ingredient to ensure that every bite is bursting with flavor. From our homemade tomato sauce to our premium toppings sourced from local farms, we guarantee a mouth-watering pizza every time.
So come join us at Puzzle Pizza and let us take you on a culinary journey like no other. Your taste buds will thank you!
